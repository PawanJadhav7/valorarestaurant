# Valora AI SaaS Success Metrics

## Overview
Valora AI measures product success using a set of **business-impact metrics** rather than only technical KPIs.

These metrics demonstrate the value of the platform to:

- restaurant operators
- investors
- enterprise customers

The goal is to clearly quantify how Valora improves restaurant performance.

---

# Core Product Impact Metrics

## 1. Revenue Impact

Measures how Valora influences restaurant revenue growth.

### Example Indicators

- Revenue uplift %
- Average order value improvement
- Customer frequency increase

Example benchmark:
Revenue uplift: +6–10% after 90 days


---

## 2. Cost Reduction Impact

Measures improvements in operational efficiency.

Primary areas include:

- food cost
- labor cost
- waste reduction
- inventory optimization

Example benchmark:
Prime cost reduction: −5–8%
Food waste reduction: −12%

---

## 3. Decision Automation Rate

Valora aims to automate operational decision-making.

Examples of automated insights:

- inventory reorder alerts
- labor optimization suggestions
- menu pricing recommendations
- anomaly detection

Example benchmark:
35% of operational decisions supported by Valora insights

---

## 4. Operator Time Saved

Restaurants spend significant time manually analyzing reports.

Valora reduces the time required to monitor operations.

Example benchmark:
Before Valora: ~6 hours/week
After Valora: ~30 minutes/week

Time saved:
≈5.5 hours/week per restaurant

---

## 5. Predictive Accuracy

AI-driven modules provide predictions and forecasts.

Key areas include:

- demand forecasting
- inventory depletion prediction
- staffing needs

Example benchmark:
Demand forecast accuracy: ~87%
Inventory prediction accuracy: ~90%

---

## 6. Module Adoption Rate

Measures how many Valora modules are used by each tenant.

Example modules:

- Sales & Demand
- Cost Control
- Menu Optimization
- Inventory Insights
- Labor Intelligence
- AI Forecasting

Example metric:
Average modules used per restaurant: 3.8

Higher module adoption increases platform stickiness.

# SaaS Business Metrics

These metrics track company growth and financial health.

## Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)

Total subscription revenue generated monthly.
MRR = Active subscriptions × plan price
---

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

Cost to acquire one restaurant customer.

Includes:

- marketing
- sales
- onboarding

---

## Net Revenue Retention (NRR)

Measures expansion revenue from existing customers.
NRR = (Starting revenue + upgrades − churn) / Starting revenue
Healthy SaaS NRR target:
110% – 130%

---

## Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

Expected revenue generated by a customer over their lifecycle.

LTV = ARPU × Customer lifetime
