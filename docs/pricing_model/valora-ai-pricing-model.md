# Valora AI Pricing Model

## Overview
Valora AI follows a **multi-lever SaaS pricing strategy** optimized for restaurant operators and multi-location chains.

The pricing model is designed to scale revenue as customers grow through:

1. **Location expansion**
2. **Advanced feature adoption**
3. **Data intelligence tiers**

This approach aligns Valora's revenue growth with the **operational value delivered to restaurants**.

---

# 1. Location-Based Pricing (Primary Revenue Lever)

Restaurant businesses scale by opening **more locations**, not by adding users.

Therefore pricing is primarily based on **number of restaurant locations per tenant**.

## Example Structure

| Plan | Locations | Monthly Price |
|-----|-----------|---------------|
| Starter | 1 location | $199 |
| Growth | Up to 5 locations | $499 |
| Scale | Up to 15 locations | $999 |
| Enterprise | Unlimited | Custom |

### Why This Works

- Restaurant groups manage operations per location
- Each location generates additional operational data
- More locations increase the value of Valora insights

### Example Revenue Growth
1 Location → $199/month
5 Locations → $499/month
20 Locations → Enterprise contract

---

# 2. Feature / Module Pricing (Expansion Revenue Lever)

Valora AI is structured as **modular intelligence layers**.

Core analytics are included in the base plan, while advanced intelligence modules can be added.

## Core Modules

- Sales & Demand Analytics
- Cost Control
- Profit Dashboard
- Operations Insights
- Menu Performance
- Inventory Monitoring
- Labor Performance

## Advanced Intelligence Modules (Future Add-ons)

| Module | Example Price |
|------|---------------|
| AI Demand Forecasting | +$99 |
| Menu Optimization AI | +$79 |
| Inventory Prediction | +$99 |
| Automated Cost Alerts | +$59 |

### Benefit

This allows customers to expand Valora usage without needing to upgrade the entire plan.

It also increases **Average Revenue Per Customer (ARPU)**.

---

# 3. Data Intelligence Tier (Premium Lever)

Large restaurant chains generate large volumes of operational data and often require deeper insights.

Higher tiers unlock advanced capabilities.

## Intelligence Levels

| Tier | Capabilities |
|-----|--------------|
| Standard | KPI dashboards and operational analytics |
| Pro | Predictive forecasting and anomaly detection |
| Enterprise | Full AI intelligence + integrations + automation |

Enterprise customers may pay significantly more depending on:

- number of locations
- integrations
- custom analytics
- enterprise support

Typical enterprise SaaS contracts may range between:
$2,000 – $10,000 per month

Restaurant operators do **not purchase dashboards**.

They purchase:

- **Profit improvement**
- **Cost reduction**
- **Operational automation**
- **predictive decision support**

Therefore Valora pricing should always communicate:

> "Valora AI increases restaurant profitability and operational efficiency."
